# Aqua Probe

Description:

Aqua Probe is our second large scale project after last years can-sat entry. Aqua Probe is a fully custom designed remotley controlled submarine. Built around an ESP32 Seeed microntroller as the centeral brain, all the electronics will be housed inside a sealed accrylic tube with O-ring end caps to ensure that everything stays watertight. Aqua Probe will display live footage back to the operator to guide them through immersive underwater exploration. Aqua Probe is designed as a sub you can quickly deploy in order to investigate sealife or to examine underwater structures quickly to find maintinance issues.

Why we made Aqua Probe?

We decided to make Aqua Probe because we have always been interested in the underwater world and engineering and noticed a lack of Submarines that you can buy off the shelf that have the capabilities of Aqua Probe. So we decided that if we couldn't find one we would just have to make our own.

How to use Aqua Probe:

To use Aqua probe simply just connect the controller and video feed to your phone and place it in the water, then actrivate the propelers and fill the ballistic and you are ready to go. The submarine has a run time of approx 30 mins until you need to surface. If you exceed this limit the power will run out however due to the externam 5m depth antenna this isnt an issue as you can retrive the submarine by pullin on the cord.
